Categorize with AI

Have hundreds of saved posts? Let AI categorize them for you.

1. Scan step

CollectIn must find all your saved posts to categorize them. Start the scan and the extension will scroll down automatically to find every post. Please stay on the page while the scan is running.

2. Categorize step

With your posts scanned, CollectIn will do the hard work to categorize them for you. It may take a minute or two to complete, so please be patient.

3. Adjust categories

Not happy with the categories? No problem. You can adjust them to your liking. Hold the category name to edit it. Move posts between categories by dragging and dropping (coming soon).

4. Save and enjoy

That's it! You can now enjoy your organized posts. Click on the "Apply categorization and reload page" to see your posts organized in the new categories.
