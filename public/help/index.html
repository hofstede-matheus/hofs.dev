Help - CollectIn

Learn how to use CollectIn to organize your LinkedIn saved posts.

Click on the extension in Chrome to open this page anytime.

Features

Set a collection

After saving a post, you'll be prompted to add it to a collection.

Filter posts by collection

Use collection filters on the saved posts and articles page to quickly find what you need.

Create a new collection

Create new collections instantly by clicking the "+" pill in LinkedIn's filter list.

Rename an existing collection

Rename an existing collection by long-pressing on the collection name in the filter list.

Remove a collection

Long-press a collection name and click the trash icon. Posts will move to "Uncategorized" collection.

Update a collection from a saved post

Click on the collection name of a saved post to change its collection.

Get organized in minutes

Install the extension and bring order to your LinkedIn saves.

FAQ

Does this change my LinkedIn account?

No. It visually augments the Saved page in your browser. Nothing is posted to your profile.

Is it free?

Yes. The core features are free.
