Rename an existing collection by long-pressing on the collection name in the filter list.

FAQ

How do I install the extension?

Simply click the "Add to Chrome" button on this page, which will take you to the Chrome Web Store. Click "Add to Chrome" and the extension will be installed automatically. No account creation or setup required.

Does this change my LinkedIn account or access my login?

No. The extension only enhances the visuals of your saved posts page in your browser and never accesses, stores, or transmits your LinkedIn login credentials. Nothing is posted to your profile, your account remains unchanged, and it does not interact with LinkedIn's authentication system.

Where is my data stored?

All your collections and post organization data is stored locally in your browser's storage. No data is sent to external servers, and no account is required. Your privacy is completely protected.

Will this slow down LinkedIn?

No. CollectIn is designed to integrate seamlessly with LinkedIn's native interface without affecting performance. The extension only activates on your saved posts page and blends naturally with LinkedIn's existing UI.

What happens to my collections if I uninstall the extension?

If you uninstall the extension, your LinkedIn saved posts remain untouched and saved in LinkedIn. However, the collection organization data stored in your browser will be lost since it's stored locally by the extension.

Can I export my collections?

Currently, collections are stored locally in your browser. The extension focuses on providing a seamless LinkedIn experience rather than import/export functionality.

What if I encounter issues or bugs?

If you experience any problems, try refreshing your LinkedIn page first. For persistent issues, you can disable and re-enable the extension in Chrome's extension settings, or visit our help page for troubleshooting guidance.
